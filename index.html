Pruning flower trees

Pruning is the process of cutting away dead or overgrown branches or stems to promote healthy plant growth.

Tree topping

Tree topping is the removal of large amounts of leaves and branches from a tree's crown.

Control of weeds, pests

Weed control is a type of pest control, which attempts to stop or reduce growth of weeds, especially noxious weeds.

Mulching

Mulch will reduce the amount of water that evaporates from your soil, greatly reducing your need to water your plants.

Mowing

Mowing is an act of leveling or cutting down grass, grain, etc., with a mowing machine or scythe.

Planting seeds

Sowing is a process of planting seeds into the soil.
